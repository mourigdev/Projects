Best Places To See In Ifran

  • The Ifrane National Park

    The park offers an experience to its visitors to see wolves, wild pigs, and various rodent species. Besides, there are diverse bird species in the park.

  • See the Large Lion Sculpture

    The Lion Sculpture is one of Ifrane’s most photographed landmarks. The sculpture was carved during the Second World War by a German soldier.

  • Skiing at Michlifen Ski Resort

    Activities like skiing, sledding, and snowboarding are popular at Michlifen Ski Resort, during the winter season. Visitors can enjoy playing in the snow.
