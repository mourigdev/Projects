Best Places To See In Ifran

  • The Ifrane National Park

    The park offers an experience to its visitors to see wolves, wild pigs, and various rodent species. Besides, there are diverse bird species in the park.

  • See the Large Lion Sculpture

    The Lion Sculpture is one of Ifrane’s most photographed landmarks. The sculpture was carved during the Second World War by a German soldier.

  • Skiing at Michlifen Ski Resort

    Activities like skiing, sledding, and snowboarding are popular at Michlifen Ski Resort, during the winter season. Visitors can enjoy playing in the snow.

Ifrane natural version

Do you dream of getting away from it all in the heart of nature? In Ifrane, experience a real moment of change of scenery thanks to the richness of its natural sites: practice agritourism, observe its unique flora and fauna, relax on the shores of large lakes such as Dayet Aoua.

What if you decide to organize a one-of-a-kind picnic! Take a tour of the natural sites near Ifrane. Lakes, springs, waterfalls and forests are as many places as they are relaxing experiences. The Atlas cedar is majestic: the cedar that grows there is endemic to Morocco.

In winter, the Michlifen or Jbel Hibri ski resort will leave you with indelible memories! Skiing, sledding or just a snowman, get ready to experience unforgettable moments!

Why Will I Invest in Ifrane?

Here are the top 4 reasons to invest in Ifrane

The number of tourists in the city of Ifrane exceeds one million visitors annually, in addition to its estimated population of 73,782 people

Al Akhawayn University: Normally the campus is closed to non-students, but there are occasional events that allow visitors to see the campus. You can also request special permission from the faculty

Centre-ville: The newer part of the city. Here, you can find the center for tourism, the iconic Lion. statue that many tourists like to take pictures next to, the lake and park, and a number of restaurants, cafes, and convenience stores.

Marché: The marché or souq, open every day, is where the locals buy their supplies. Anything from books and electronics to meat and fruit can be bought here. There are also many restaurants of varying quality and price.
